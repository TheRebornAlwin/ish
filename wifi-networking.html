Who Needs This?

Anyone with dead zones, dropouts, buffering, or 50+ connected devices (smart homes need robust enterprise-grade WiFi)

Technology We Use

Ubiquiti UniFi, Ruckus, Cisco Meraki - enterprise access points, managed switches, VLAN segmentation for security

Real Example

6-bed new build in Newport - 12 UniFi access points, wired backhaul, separate VLANs for IoT/guests/security, 10Gb backbone for 4K streaming

Our Approach
