Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Infinity Smart Home Systems uses cookies to:

  • Ensure our website functions properly
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how you use our website to improve user experience
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Provide personalized content

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions you make, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve how our website works.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and choices.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your browsing habits to show you advertising that is more relevant to you and your interests. They may be set by us or third-party partners.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that also set cookies on your device. These may include:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media platforms for social sharing features
  • Marketing and advertising platforms

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Cookie Duration

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device until they expire or you delete them).

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Important Note

Please be aware that blocking or deleting cookies may impact your user experience and some features of our website may not function properly.

7. Browser-Specific Cookie Management

For instructions on how to manage cookies in specific browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

8. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business practices. Please check this page regularly for the latest information.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through the contact form on our website.
